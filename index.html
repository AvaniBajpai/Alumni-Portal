WELCOME TO SARC

SARC provides platform for students to interact with well settled alumni's of NITJ. It aids in gaining career advice and gives them direction towards better future. SARC also organise alumni meet every year. It is a one day get together of all the passed out batches that involves various fun and productive events alongside a round table interaction with the esteemed Director and other respected dignitaries.

Vision

  • Rendering students with career guidance and tailoring them for their future endeavours
  • Catering to the betterment of alumni-student relationship of Institute
  • To furnish alumni with a platform to interact with the students on a one to one basis and connect them with the Institute and provide career advice to prepare for the real world
